Our Mission

Haryana Bus Info exists to make public transport information in Haryana accessible to everyone — for free, without ads cluttering the experience, and without requiring an app download.

Millions of people across Haryana depend on Haryana Roadways every day. Finding bus timings shouldn't require standing at a bus stand asking strangers. We built this so you can check from your phone before you leave home.

How We Collect Data

Our timetable data is collected through an automated pipeline and cleaned by hand before publishing. Here's how it works:

🌐
Automated Scraping
A Python + Playwright scraper collects raw schedule data from official Haryana Roadways sources. Multithreaded for speed with MD5-based deduplication.
🧹
Data Cleaning
Station names are normalized and fuzzy-matched using rapidfuzz to fix spelling variants. Departure times are standardized to HH:MM 24-hour format.
🔄
Deduplication
A merge script compares records using normalized time logic to remove false duplicates while preserving all genuine schedule entries.
✅
Manual Review
Ambiguous records flagged by the pipeline are reviewed manually before being added to the live database.
🚀
Published to Site
Cleaned data is uploaded to the WordPress-based timetable database and served at haryanabusinfo.in.
Frequently Asked Questions
Is this an official Haryana Roadways website?
No. This is an independent, community-maintained resource. We are not affiliated with Haryana Roadways or the Haryana government. For official information, visit the Haryana Roadways official portal.
How current is the schedule data?
We update the database regularly as Haryana Roadways changes its timetables. However, schedules can change without notice — always verify critical timings at your local bus stand.
Why is my bus route not showing up?
We are continuously adding more routes. If a route is missing, you can report it via the main site at haryanabusinfo.in and we'll add it in the next update.
Is this website free?
Yes — completely free, no sign-up required, no app to download. It works on any phone browser, including 2G connections.
Can I use this data for my own project?
Please contact us via the main site first. We're open to data partnerships for genuinely public-interest use cases.
