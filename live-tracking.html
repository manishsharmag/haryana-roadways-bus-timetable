Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Haryana bus tracking

Can I track Haryana Roadways buses in real time?
Yes. Haryana Roadways has GPS units installed on a large portion of its fleet. Live tracking shows approximate bus location and estimated arrival for key routes across Haryana.
How accurate is the Haryana bus GPS tracking?
GPS tracking is typically accurate within 2–5 minutes. Traffic conditions, stops, and diversions may cause variations. Always check the timetable at haryanabusinfo.in for scheduled departure times.
Which routes have live tracking available?
Most major inter-district routes including Delhi, Chandigarh, Rohtak, Hisar, Karnal and Ambala corridors have GPS-enabled buses. Coverage on smaller intra-district routes is expanding.
How do I find the Haryana bus timetable?
Visit haryanabusinfo.in for the complete Haryana Roadways schedule with all departure times and route details.

Haryana Roadways Live Bus Tracking

Haryana Roadways has deployed GPS tracking units across a significant portion of its bus fleet, enabling real-time monitoring of bus locations on major routes. This live tracking page shows the current position of buses on key corridors including Rohtak-Delhi, Karnal-Chandigarh, Hisar-Sirsa, Sonipat-Panipat, and more.

Track Your Bus by Route Number

Every Haryana Roadways bus is assigned a route number. Enter this number in the tracker above to see which stop the bus is currently at, how many stops remain before your station, and the estimated arrival time. Route numbers are displayed at bus stands and on the front panel of the bus.

Haryana Roadways Fleet GPS Coverage

Haryana Roadways operates a fleet of over 4,000 buses across the state. GPS devices have been fitted on express, long-distance, and Volvo AC buses in priority. Ordinary local route buses are being progressively covered. For the most accurate scheduled timings on any route, use the timetable search at haryanabusinfo.in.
