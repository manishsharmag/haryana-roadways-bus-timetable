About Haryana Roadways Bus Routes

This directory lists Haryana Roadways bus routes connecting cities across all 22 districts of Haryana. Every route links directly to the full schedule at haryanabusinfo.in showing all departure times, via stops, and bus type information.

Types of Haryana Roadways Buses

Ordinary buses serve local and short-distance routes with multiple stops. Express buses cover inter-district routes with fewer stops and faster travel times. Volvo AC buses operate on premium long-distance routes including Haryana to Delhi, Chandigarh, and other major destinations.

How to Read the Haryana Roadways Timetable

Each route listing shows the origin, destination, and major via stops. Click any route to see the full timetable at haryanabusinfo.in — including all scheduled departure times, the operating depot, and days of service. Timings are in 24-hour format (e.g., 14:30 = 2:30 PM).
