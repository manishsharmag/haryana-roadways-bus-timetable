About Haryana Roadways

Haryana Roadways, officially the Haryana State Transport, is the government-owned bus service of Haryana, India. It operates thousands of bus routes connecting cities, towns, and villages across the state. The fleet includes Ordinary buses for local routes, Express buses for inter-city travel, and Volvo AC coaches for premium long-distance routes including Delhi and Chandigarh.
